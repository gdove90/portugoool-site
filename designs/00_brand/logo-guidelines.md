# Logo Guidelines

The primary mark is the **wordmark**: PORTUGOOOL set in the display face,
with the three O's ("OOO") in Match Red against ink or paper.

## Wordmark Construction
- `PORTUG` + `OOO` (red) + `L` — the stretched O's ARE the brand gesture
  (the sound of the goal held long).
- Always uppercase, always tight tracking. Never letter-spaced.

## Variants (to be produced → stored in `designs/12_logos/`)
- [ ] Light: ink wordmark / red OOO — for paper backgrounds
- [ ] Dark: paper wordmark / red OOO — for ink/green backgrounds
- [ ] Gold: single-color gold — premium/packaging contexts only
- [ ] Monogram/social avatar: standalone "OOO" or "G-OOO-L" lockup (TBD)

## Clearspace & Minimum Size
- Clearspace: height of the "O" on all sides.
- Minimum size: legible at 120px wide on screen / 30mm in print (verify when
  final files exist).

## Misuse (never)
- Never stretch, skew, outline, or add shadows/gradients
- Never recolor the OOO outside red or gold
- Never place on busy photography without a scrim
- Never combine with federation crests, flags-as-official-marks, FIFA or
  sportswear-brand elements

## Spelling Reference
- Brand: **PORTUGOOOL** (4 O's total: PORTUG-OOO-L)
- Repo/Netlify slug: `portugoool` (3 O's) — infrastructure only, never in
  customer-facing copy.
