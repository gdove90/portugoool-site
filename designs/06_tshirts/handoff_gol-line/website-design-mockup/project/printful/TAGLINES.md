# PORTUGOOOL · Drop 01 — Tagline Bank

Voice rules (from brand guide): short, emotional, premium, direct.
Loud ideas, quiet sentences. `·` as separator. No hype overload,
no fake urgency. PT carries the soul; EN travels; ES joins the chant.

## Master lines
- **The Sound of Victory.** — brand line, EN (locked)
- **O som da vitória.** — brand line, PT (locked, on T3 back)
- **Ouves?** — "Do you hear it?" (locked, T3 front)

## Drop / campaign lines

- **500 de cada. Depois, silêncio.** — *500 of each. Then, silence.*
- **When it's gone, it's gone.** (carried from Drop I banner)

## Usage notes
- One line per surface. Never stack two taglines.
- PT lines ship with no translation on garments; translations live in
  product descriptions only.
- "Verão '26" is the only approved tournament-adjacent phrase — never
  "World Cup", "Copa", or "Mundial" in drop copy.
