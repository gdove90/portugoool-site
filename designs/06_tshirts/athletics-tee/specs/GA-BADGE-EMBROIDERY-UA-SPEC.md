# GOOOL Athletics — GA Badge Embroidery Spec (UA Tee)

Received 2026-07-23. Verbatim production spec.

## Product
- Printful product: Men's Under Armour Athletic T-Shirt | 1383264 (catalog id 773)
- Decoration: standard flat embroidery, left chest
- One color · no 3D puff · no unlimited color · no printed background · no patch effect

## Final badge size
- Finished: **2.50 in w × 2.75 in h** (aspect 0.91:1 — never stretch)
- Centered in Printful's left-chest embroidery area

## Artwork content
Use ONLY: (1) the simplified outer shield, (2) the approved intertwined GA
monogram. NO: GOOOL text, ATHLETICS text, stars, extra borders, gradients,
shadows, distressing, fine lines, background fill, extra lettering.

## Shield construction
- Rounded professional soccer crest: slight upward top curve, rounded upper
  corners, sides taper inward, single centered bottom point
- No fill — outline only, solid black, one continuous closed shape
- Outline thickness: min 0.10 in · recommended 0.11 in
- No double outline; no thinning near the bottom point

## GA monogram
- Exact approved intertwined geometry (see designs/12_logos/athletics/GA-MONOGRAM-SPEC.md)
- G dominant upper left; A extends through lower right; one connected monogram
- No typed letters, no separation, no outline around letters
- Stroke: min 0.16 in · recommended 0.18 in
- Min open counter/negative space: 0.25 in
- Min monogram-to-shield gap: 0.25 in
- Simplify any feature below these limits

## Monogram placement
- ~1.65 in w × ~1.70 in h
- Centered horizontally; visually centered vertically (~0.05 in above
  mathematical center); never touches the shield; equal left/right breathing room

## Vector master
Retain SVG + PDF + EPS master: closed paths, united overlaps, no live
fonts/clipping masks/hidden objects/stray points. The upload PNG must be
exported from this master.

## Digitization (instructions to Printful)
- Standard flat apparel embroidery
- Satin stitch: shield outline + narrow GA strokes
- Tatami fill only where satin would exceed provider max width
- Underlay/stabilizer appropriate for 60/40 cotton-poly athletic fabric
- Priorities: clean shield edges, open counters, clear G/A separation,
  minimal puckering, consistent density, sharp reinforced bottom point
- Shield interior stays unfilled — fabric shows through

## Placement
- Wearer's left chest, ~3.25 in below shoulder/collar junction,
  ~3.5–4 in from garment center line
- Printful left-chest placement template is the final authority
- Not directly beneath collar; not toward armpit; never enlarge past
  2.50 in wide without approval

## Quality control (pre-production checklist)
- [ ] 2.50 × 2.75 in
- [ ] One thread color
- [ ] Shield outline ≥ 0.10 in
- [ ] Monogram strokes ≥ 0.16 in
- [ ] Key negative spaces ≥ 0.25 in
- [ ] G and A visibly intertwined; A counter open
- [ ] Shield interior unfilled
- [ ] Bottom point reinforced and clean
- [ ] Centered in left-chest area
- [ ] No text or stars added; no proportions changed
- Embroidery approval sheet before production if artwork was adjusted;
  never auto-simplify the GA monogram without approval
